Free Trial Account FAQ
Account
What are the limitations of my trial account?
Trial accounts are not intended for commercial use. Trial accounts expire after 45-days and include 1 human user, 1 bot user, and 1000 conversations per month. To continue using your account with richer features and live customer support, upgrade to a paid account.
How can I upgrade my free trial account?
Chat with Sales from liveperson.com or from the Connection Area/Contact LivePerson Tab inside of LiveEngage and a conversational specialist will help you to choose and upgrade to the right LiveEngage package for your business.
Tools
I'm a developer. What sort of tools does LivePerson provide?
LivePerson offers a suite of 40+ APIs and several SDKs that allow you to deeply customize LiveEngage behaviors, create custom integrations, and more. LivePerson's Functions lets you write and deploy serverless code that can trigger off of conversational events.
Products
I want to use a mobile channel other than SMS or Facebook. What do I do?
We support a variety of mobile channels, such as Apple Business Chat, WhatsApp, and Line, to LiveEngage subscribers. To upgrade your account, contact sales.
