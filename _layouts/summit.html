LivePerson's Co/Development Summit

LiveEngage's Open Platform offers our customers the unique opportunity to create the perfect consumer and agent experience. Join our experts for a rewarding and intensive 24 hours of cooperative development to unlock our platform's full potential!

Join us!

Join us at Google's prestigious Campus in Tel Aviv where we'll be joining forces to tackle issues like:

  • Agent Survey - develop a solution to better poll your agents for their pain points and quality of life needs.
  • Post-Engagement Survey - enable your agents to send customized post-engagement surveys on the fly.
  • Status Bot - build, deploy and maintain efficient status bots to keep your agent managers aware and present.
  • Dashboard - design and launch useful dashboards, with all your contact center's information in one place.
  • Reporting Corner - dazzled by too many reports? Collect them in one place for ease of use and visibility.
  • Routing - dig deeper into LiveEngage's routing capabilities to get the right visitor at the right time to the right agent.

Our developer community has documentation on the many APIs and SDKs we'll be using during the Summit. Check out this video above to start getting acquainted with them in advance!
