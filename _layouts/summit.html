Development Summit

We invite you to Tel Aviv on November 15th - 16th, for a summit bringing together developers, product designers and LivePerson's R&D.

Join us!
What will you achieve at the Tel Aviv Summit?

The LiveEngage Open Platform offers our customers the unique opportunity to enhance the consumer care experience. Join our experts for an intensive 24 hours of cooperative development!

Your influence will help shape the products that your business will later use. Help create market-ready products and take exclusive advantage of them right away.

What You Need To Know
Who - You'll be building a product end to end. Developers, product designers, and managers are all welcome to contribute their own unique perspective.
What - Your team. Our developers. 24 hours. Joining forces. Building the next generation of consumer care products.
Where - Tel Aviv, the innovation capital of Israel.
When - November 15th-16th.

From our hands to yours, each team will receive access to top-of-the-line knowledge and experience in order to develop solutions like:

  • Agent Surveys - develop a solution to better poll your agents for their quality of life needs and pain points.
  • Post-Engagement Surveys - enable your agents to send customized post-engagement surveys on the fly.
  • Dashboards - design and launch a useful dashboard, encompassing all your contact center's KPIs.
  • Routing Bot - create an efficient bot that routes your visiors to the right skill and the right agent at the right time.
