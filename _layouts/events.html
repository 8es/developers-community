LivePerson offers bot makers and brands the world’s first “bot lifecycle platform”

LiveEngage for Bots is the first comprehensive platform to launch, manage, secure and optimize bots at a massive scale. We’ve made our wide array of APIs and SDKs available to bot builders to develop human-like bot experiences that consumers love and large brands can scale across their customer base.

Adding your bot to the LiveEngage platform means you can take advantage of our 20 years of experience in providing customer-service technology used by the world’s top brands.

Develop your bot on our bot platform and you can enjoy all of the benefits our advanced technology brings:
  • Deployment across our messaging channels: SMS, Facebook, Web Messaging & In-app Messaging
  • Real time sentiment analysis and reporting
  • Queue management and advanced routing
  • Conversation management, including escalation to human agents and transfer back and forth
  • Secure access to our backend systems via integration engines
  • Use of our bot studio to design and track bots
  • Detailed analytics and reports on bot performance
